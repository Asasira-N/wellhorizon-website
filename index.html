About Us

WellHorizon Research Initiative Ltd (WHRI) is a youth-founded, evidence-based organisation improving health and wellbeing in Uganda. We strengthen community health practices, advance environmental health research, and promote holistic wellness grounded in compassion, stewardship, and dignity. As a non-profit, WHRI focuses on reducing preventable illness, especially in rural and underserved communities.
